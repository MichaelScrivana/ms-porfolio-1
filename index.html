01

One A Day — Orchestrated Design Review Agent

Challenge

Design managers review hundreds of agency submissions for brand compliance — a manual, time-intensive process that limits strategic focus and creates bottlenecks across the portfolio.

Solution

Built an orchestrated AI agent on Azure with Claude. Agencies submit designs to a web app where the review agent scores submissions against brand guidelines with built-in compliance guardrails. The system is modular and designed to scale across all BCH brands.

Automation Vision

The endgame is full automation of executional volume — digital banners, social templates, ecommerce tiles — generated by AI with brand compliance baked in. Next phase: a generative design agent that pulls brand assets and creates compliant designs, freeing design managers from reviewing hundreds of executions so they can focus on strategic brand vision.

03

GenAI in Creative Workflows

Challenge

Traditional creative development cycles — from concept exploration to photography direction to asset production — are time-intensive and limit the volume of ideas a team can explore.

Solution

Pioneered MidJourney as a production tool across BCH brands. Used it to develop photography style guides that aligned agency partners on creative direction, generated packaging concepts, key visuals, and ecommerce assets — both hands-on and directing agency partners to adopt AI into their workflows.

Impact

Dramatically accelerated creative ideation. Shortened the gap between vision and execution. Proved that GenAI can enhance — not replace — the creative process.
